## ✅ SOLUTION SECTION

**SlugSwap creates a fair, pooled system where giving and receiving feels natural.**

No awkward asks. No tracking debts. Just a community that takes care of each other.

**Pooled Weekly Support:** Your monthly donation is split into weekly pools. Everyone draws from the same community fund—no individual transactions, no pressure.

**Fair Weekly Allowances:** Request what you need, when you need it. Everyone gets equal access to their weekly allowance. First-come-first-served, but reset every week so everyone gets a fair shot.

**Instant Claim Codes:** Generate a secure code through your school's GET Tools system in seconds. Redeem at any campus dining location immediately. No waiting, no complicated transfers.

**Complete Transparency:** See the pool balance, weekly distributions, and community impact. Know your contribution is making a real difference without tracking individual recipients.

**Privacy Protected:** Donors don't see who requests. Just a community supporting each other, anonymously and with dignity.

### For Donors:

Set it and forget it. Your support flows to those who need it most, every single week.

Use your code at any dining hall or campus café. Get the meal you need, no questions asked.

## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Is my information private?**
A: Completely. Donors never see who requests points. We protect everyone's dignity and privacy.

**Q: What if I need to request points one week and donate the next?**
A: You can be both! SlugSwap isn't about roles—it's about community. Help when you can, request when you need. That's how mutual aid works.

**Q: How do I know the pool won't run out?**
A: Weekly pools reset every Monday. Donations are divided across the month, so there's a steady flow. Plus, we show real-time pool balances so you always know what's available.

**Q: Are claim codes safe?**
A: Yes. Codes are generated through your official campus GET Tools account, expire in minutes, and are single-use only. This prevents abuse while keeping redemption instant.

**Q: What happens to unused donations?**
A: Unused points from each week roll into the next week's pool. Nothing goes to waste—it all supports students who need meals.

**Q: Can I change my donation amount?**
A: Anytime. Increase, decrease, or pause your contributions whenever your situation changes. No commitment, no judgment.
